** Example 1
$3y(x+4+7^2)$
www.haider.gq/maths/Algebraic-products/Exp-Brack-1.png
*** Methord
We solve this by going left to right.

1. $3y \times x = 3yx$
2. $3y \times 4 = 12y$
3. $3y \times 7^2 = 3y7^2$

   
$3y(x+4+7^2)$
