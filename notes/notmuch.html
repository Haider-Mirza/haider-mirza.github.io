Notmuch

About

Notmuch is just a email system that is incredibly fast and is based off tagging. Notmuch also has a website.

You can find my configuration in my Dotfiles repository (I use Notmuch's Emacs frontend). Note that this has been highly configured to my needs and is not made to be a drop-in-replacement for you; It's just a reference. My personal email is rerouted using an alias that is managed by Cloudflare.

How to attach a file

The way is the exact same to how you do it in Mu4e. When composing, go into the text area and do the command C-c C-a to attach a file.
