A Email alias is a Email address that just reroutes to a existing mailbox. like my email address:

Alias Actual mailbox
haider@haider.gq foo@foo.com (e.g test@gmail.com)

My Website's (haider.gq) DNS is managed by Cloudflare DNS and my Alias is created through Cloudflare.
