Aerobic Respiration

Aerobic Respiration is a type of Respiration that releases a large amount of energy and requires oxygen.

In the human body

Anerobic Respiration is a chemical

glucose + oxygen → carbon dioxide + water + (A bit of heat, not a feasable thing) \(C_6{}H_12{}O_6 + 6O_2 → 6CO_2 + 6H_2{}O\)

This heat which is generated is what makes us as humans warm blooded. Aerobic Respiration occurs Mitochondria organelle.
