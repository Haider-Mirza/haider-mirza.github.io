Work

Work

Work is a force causing the displacement of object and is measured In Joules. Come on man, Joules is so much better than horsepower. Be greatful!

Formula

The formula for work is the following:

\(W = F \times d\)

Force is mass \(\times\) acceleration. \(F = M \times A\)

The work(w) done on an object would be equal to the force(f) applied to it multiplied by the distance(d) it travelled.
