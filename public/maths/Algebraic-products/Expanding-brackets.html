    Example 1

    \(3y(x+4+7^2)\)

    Methord

    We solve this by going left to right.

    1. \(3y \times x = 3yx\)
    2. \(3y \times 4 = 12y\)
    3. \(3y \times 7^2 = 3y7^2\)

    You can see this procedure below

    And then you collect all of the outputs and then you have expanded your brackets.

    \(3y(x+4+7^2)\) = \(3yx+12y+3y7^2\)
